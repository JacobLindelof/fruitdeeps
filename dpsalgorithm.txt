max hit:
	ranged/melee:
		0.5 + A*(B+64)/640
		A:
			boosted strength
			apply prayer
			add stance
			apply void
		B:
			ranged/melee strength bonus				

		after:
			apply black mask/salve
		

	Magic:
		if slayerdart, trident, salamandar, sang:
			calc max hit
		else if spell: lookup table
		apply charge, and or chaos gauntlet bonus
		
		apply magic dmg % bonus (including salve, void)
		apply black mask
		apply tome of fire

	B:
